About Biodiversity Park

Biodiversity Park, located in Ranchi, Jharkhand, is a haven for nature lovers and environmental enthusiasts. Established to conserve and showcase the region's rich plant diversity, the park features a wide variety of indigenous and exotic plant species. It serves as an educational and recreational destination, promoting awareness about the importance of biodiversity and ecological balance. The park is a popular spot for families, students, and tourists seeking tranquility and a deeper connection with nature.
