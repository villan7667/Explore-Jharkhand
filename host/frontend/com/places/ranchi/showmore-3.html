Rock Garden

Discover the artistic beauty and serene environment of Ranchi's Rock Garden

History of Rock Garden

Rock Garden in Ranchi is a masterpiece of creativity and nature. Built using recycled materials, it showcases sculptures and designs that blend seamlessly with the natural landscape. The garden is a testament to the artistic vision of its creators and has become a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.
