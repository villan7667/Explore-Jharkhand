History of Dassam Falls

Dassam Falls, located in Jharkhand, India, is a natural wonder with a rich history. The falls, formed by the Kanchi River, cascade down from a height of 144 feet, creating a spectacular view that has attracted visitors for centuries.Dasham Falls is not just a natural wonder, but also a cultural treasure. The name Dasham or Dassam means “ten” in the local Mundari language, and it refers to the legend that the waterfall was created by ten brothers who were cursed by a goddess for their sins.
