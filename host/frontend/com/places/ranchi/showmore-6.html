Jagannath Temple

Discover the spiritual and architectural marvel of Ranchi's iconic Jagannath Temple

History of
Jagannath Temple

Jagannath Temple in Ranchi, Jharkhand, is a revered Hindu temple built in 1691 by King Ani Nath Shahdeo. Modeled after the famous Jagannath Temple of Puri, it stands atop a hill, offering panoramic views of the city. The temple is renowned for its annual Rath Yatra, attracting thousands of devotees. Despite suffering damage in 1990, it was restored to its former glory, preserving its unique blend of architectural beauty and spiritual significance.
