Patratu Valley

Experience the serene beauty of Jharkhand's picturesque valley

History of Patratu Valley

Patratu Valley, located approximately 40 kilometers from Ranchi in Jharkhand, is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, lush greenery, and meandering roads. The valley offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Patratu Dam, situated nearby, adds to the valley's charm, providing opportunities for boating and other water activities. The area is also home to the Patratu Thermal Power Station, a significant contributor to the region's energy supply.
