Bokaro City Park & Lake

Relax and unwind at Bokaro's most beautiful urban oasis

History of City Park & Lake

City Park and Lake, located in Bokaro Steel City, Jharkhand, is a popular recreational spot known for its scenic beauty and tranquil environment. Developed as part of the city's urban planning, the park features a large artificial lake, lush gardens, and walking trails. Over the years, it has become a favorite destination for families, tourists, and nature lovers, offering boating, picnics, and cultural events amidst a peaceful setting.
