History of Garga Dam

Garga Dam is a major reservoir and watercourse located in Bokaro district, Jharkhand. The dam is located on the Garga river, about 12 km from Bokaro town, and is crucial for regional water supply and irrigation. The Garga Dam was constructed in the 1970s and has become a major source of water supply to Bokaro and surrounding areas.The main purpose of the Garga Dam is to ensure water supply to the Bokaro region and adjoining areas.
