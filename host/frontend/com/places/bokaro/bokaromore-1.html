Garga Dam

Discover the tranquility and scenic beauty of Bokaro's iconic Garga Dam

History of Garga Dam

Garga Dam, located near Bokaro Steel City in Jharkhand, is one of the oldest dams in the region. Built across the Garga River in the 1950s, it was constructed to supply water to the Bokaro Steel Plant and the city. Over the years, Garga Dam has become a popular picnic spot, attracting visitors with its serene environment, lush greenery, and opportunities for bird watching and boating. The dam stands as a testament to the region's industrial development and natural charm.
